Computer Science and Engineering

Computer Science and Engineering is a four-year degree programme offered at Diploma, Undergraduate, and Postgraduate levels. Computer Science Engineering is a sub-discipline of Engineering that deals with the design, implementation, and management of information systems of both software and hardware processes.
