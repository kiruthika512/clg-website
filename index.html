Welcome to Saveetha

Saveetha Engineering College is a private, autonomous college affiliated to Anna University. Saveetha Engineering College, Chennai is approved by AICTE. The college is accredited by the NBA. SEC Chennai is accredited by NAAC with an A grade. Courses offered by SEC Chennai include BE, BTech, MBA, ME, MTech, MS and PhD in different disciplines.Saveetha Engineering College (SEC) is an engineering college located in Chennai, India, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering.The college has a total strength of 4349 students in 12 UG courses, including Agricultural, Artificial Intelligence & Data Sciences, Biomedical Engineering, Civil, Chemical, CSE, ECE, EEE, EIE, IT, Mechanical, and Medical Electronics.
